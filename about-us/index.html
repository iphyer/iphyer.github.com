About Me

Sep 23rd, 2012

Hello everyone!I’m waventropy,a student of Physics department at Nanjing University.

The name waventropy is inspired from the concept of wave and entropy, both are the most fundamental and basic idea in physics.I have been studying physics in Nanjing University for nearly 3 years.I like physics even though I find the study is both difficult and in demand of much hard work. Sometimes I am frustrated to admit the fact that physics really requires some rare talents especially when your performanceis limited comparing to other students. However, I still enjoy the feeling of being challenged. Life can not be sunny all the time,so the best best way to live is keeping struggling. Besides physics, I have chosen many other courses like math, computer and biology which are also engrossing when I further study.

My hometown is Yangzhou, a beautiful and fascinating city in Jiangsu Province. The city is founded in 486 BC by Fucha, the king of Wu during late Spring and Autumn period in China. Yangzhou will celebrate her 2500th anniversary in 2014. During her long history,Yangzhou had been the most significant city for the Beijing-Hangzhou Grand Canal because of her unique geographical advantages. For ancient China, Yangzhou was a city like Shanghai for modern China.

Hope you can have a wonderful experience about this website!
